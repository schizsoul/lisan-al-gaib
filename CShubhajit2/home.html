Hello, I'm Shubhajit.

I'm a content developer & editor based in India. I produce strategic marketing content for start-ups, tech companies, and VC Firms.

I help content marketers, product marketers, and executives tell stories, achieve online presence, and build brands that communicate with their audiences.

My Services

Blog Writing

Blogs are not just hordes of content but essentially a marketing tool and more importantly, a part of your branding strategy.

I write blogs, not necessarily based on SEO or trendy topic, but from a branding perspective. I have expertise in writing a variety of topics for a number of clients, and I always try to match my voice with the company's voice.

Social Media Marketing

It is important to add the edge of maturity and clarity to your digital strategies. I know the technicalities of paid campaigns in most of the social media channels, but it is not what I am selling.

I am selling concepts, advertising campaigns and persuasive copy that will enlarge your business’s footprint and sharpen your social media vision.

Content Strategy

I develop a content strategy based on your business objectives. I plan, design, create, write and edit content for the brand's designated audience.

I first take a brief from you and understand your business goals and your target audience's needs, then helps plan, develop and deliver clear, relevant content that brings the two together.
